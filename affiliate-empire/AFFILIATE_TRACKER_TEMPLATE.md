## 📊 Conversion Rate Benchmarks

### Typical Conversion Rates:
- **Cold Traffic** (Google): 1-3%
- **Warm Traffic** (Social, YouTube): 3-5%
- **Hot Traffic** (Email List): 5-15%
- **Retargeting Ads**: 10-20%

### Your Goal:
- Start: 1-2% conversion rate
- Month 3: 3-5% conversion rate
- Month 6+: 5-10% conversion rate

**How to Improve:**
- Better targeting (right audience)
- Stronger CTAs (clear value)
- Social proof (reviews, testimonials)
- Trust signals (security badges, guarantees)
- Follow-up sequences (email)
