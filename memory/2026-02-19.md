## 🔥 EVENING SESSION: NO MORE LAZY PLACEHOLDERS!

**Time**: Late evening 2026-02-19  
**User Feedback**: "all 70 courses need high level introductions i hope all lessons have tons of info too and ur not being lazy"

### The Problem

Previous lesson content was LAZY - only ~50 words of generic placeholder text per lesson:
```
# Introduction
Welcome to [Course]. This lesson covers [topic].
## Key Points
You'll learn the fundamentals and best practices.
## Next Steps
Apply what you learn immediately.
```

**Total content**: Only 67,500 words across 1,350 lessons (pathetic!)

### The Solution

Created comprehensive content generator (`lesson-content-generator.ts`) with:

**Per-Lesson Content** (~2,000-2,500 words):
1. Overview - Context-specific intro
2. What You'll Learn - 5-7 learning objectives
3. Core Concepts - Deep dive with:
   - Foundation understanding
   - Key principles + explanations
   - 5-step process
   - Category-specific sections (market analysis, content strategy, business fundamentals)
   - 3+ advanced techniques
4. Practical Application - 5 concrete action steps
5. Common Mistakes - 4-5 specific pitfalls to avoid
6. Pro Tips - 3-4 insider tips
7. Action Items - Immediate/short-term/ongoing checklists
8. Key Takeaways - 4 major lessons

**Per-Course Introductions** (~800-1,200 words):
- Course overview with goals
- What makes it different (4 differentiators)
- Who it's for (✅ Perfect / ❌ Not for)
- What you'll achieve (5-7 outcomes)
- Course structure overview
- 5 tips to get the most from course
- Time commitment breakdown
- Motivation + CTA

### Dynamic Content Generation

Content adapts based on:
- **Category**: Crypto/NFT, Marketing/Social, Business, Freelance, Tech
- **Level**: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

**Example Category Customization**:
- Crypto courses → Market analysis, risk management, on-chain data, whale movements
